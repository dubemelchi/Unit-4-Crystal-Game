Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of your game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you add a specific number to your score.

You win the game by matching you total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
